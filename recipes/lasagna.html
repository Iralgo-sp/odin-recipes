Lasagna

Description

This is the recipe of the lasagna my mum used to cook for us.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 100ml water
  • 500 gr mince meet
  • 1 onion and two cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes, 2 cans of tomato sauce and two cans of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 1 kilo of lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta cheese

Directions

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagne according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
